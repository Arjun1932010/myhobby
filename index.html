    About

    My favourite hobby is bey blading. Blading is a game in which we have to spin some or two tops and collide them without touching. They provide fun and surprises. Beyblades are of different shapes and sizes. The top which stops spinning or is out of the ring or stadium or destroyed or bursted losses but, the top which is which is not out is the winner. I have Z Achilles the main beyblade in Beyblade Burst Turbo Series. It was given to me at my birthday by my sister. I and my mother ordered it from Amazon. I fight with my friend with it. He has two but I always defeated one but, the otherone is strong. I love blading.
